| Name | Fingerprint |

```
gpg --recv-keys 7416BB255E60E40D482E591B72018930A1FB3444 --keyserver hkps://keys.openpgp.org
```

You can also download it from [our website](https://bitcoindevkit.org/foundation/pgp/#security-disclosures).
